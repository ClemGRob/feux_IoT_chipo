no priority control:
    if there is no car, both light stay or become red
    if there is a car on one of the two road, the opposit light become or stay red,
        and when it is done, the other one become green
    if there is car on both road, the lights will alternate between red end green. each will stay green during 30s (Parameters)

with priority control:
    the priority light will become green, and the not priority one will become red
    only one road can be prioritary
